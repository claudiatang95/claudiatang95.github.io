About me

I'm a first-year PhD student at the Information School of University of Wisconsin-Madison, supervised by Prof. Kyung-Sun Kim. I received my M.A. in Public and Corporate Communication (Data Analytics track) from University of Milan, where I worked with Prof. Flaminio Squazzoni, who introduced me to the field of behavioral science.

Research interests

My research interests lie at the intersection of information behavior, AI and data science. I'm particularly interested in how people use AI tools during information seeking processes, aiming to understand and inform AI's implications for human information practices.
